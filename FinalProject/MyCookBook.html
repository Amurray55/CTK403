Homemade Pork Carnitas

Ingredients:

  • 3 - 3.5 lbs - Pork Butt(Pork Shoulder)
  • 2 lbs - Manteca (Mexican Lard, can use any lard, Manetac tastes the best)
  • 2 - Oranges
  • 5 - Cloves
  • 2 - Bay leaves
  • 1 tsp - Cumin
  • 5 oz - Evaporated Milk
  • 2tsp - Mexian Oregano
  • 1 to 2 packages Tortillas (corn or flour)
  • 1 package melting cheese (chihuahua is preferred)
  • 1 spanish/sweet onion

Notes:
(General rule: 1 lb Lard for every 3 lbs of Meat) I did use 2 lbs of lard to cover more of the meat in the pot. You want 80% coverage of the meat in the lard.
Dutch Oven is preferred, but a big pot that can go in oven works as well.
