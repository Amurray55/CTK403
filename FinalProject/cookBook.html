Thai Chili Soy Salmon

Thai Chili Soy Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 1–2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2–3 tbsp sweet Thai chili sauce
  • ½ tsp garlic powder

    • Instructions:

    • 1. Make marinade Mix soy sauce, sweet Thai chili sauce, and garlic powder
    • 2. Marinate salmon Coat salmon and let sit for 30+ minutes
    • 3. Preheat air fryer Set to 380°F.
    • 4. Cook Place salmon in air fryer and cook for 11–13 minutes
    • 5. Serve Eat as-is or with rice/vegetables

    Chopped Cheese

    Chopped Cheese

    Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 for flavor)
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 2 hero rolls (or hoagie/sub rolls)
  • 2 tbsp mayo
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp yellow mustard
  • Shredded iceberg lettuce
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Pickles (optional but very Matty energy)
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Neutral oil or butter

    • Instructions:

    • 1. Cook onion + beef Cook diced onion in a pan, then add ground beef. Season with salt & pepper.
    • 2. Chop it up Break the beef into small pieces while cooking and mix with onions.
    • 3. Add cheese Put cheese on top, let it melt, then mix it into the meat.
    • 4. Add sauce Spread mayo, ketchup, and mustard on the bread.
    • 5. Toast bread/roll in pan
    • 6. Build your sandwich!

    Simple Cottage Cheese Bagels

    Simple Cottage Cheese Bagels

    Ingredients:

  • 1 cup self-rising flour
  • 1 cup full-fat cottage cheese
  • 1 large egg beaten, for egg wash
  • Toppings if wanted: sesame seeds, everything bagel seasoning, shredded asiago cheese, or poppy seeds

    • Instructions:

  • 1. Preheat the oven to 375 and position the rack to the top position. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
  • 2. Blend cottage cheese: Place the cottage cheese in the food processor or blender and pulse or blend on high until smooth, stopping to scrape down the sides as needed.
  • 3. Add flour: Transfer it to a medium bowl and add the flour. Mix until completely combined and the dough starts to pull away from the sides of the bowl. Transfer the dough to a generously floured work surface and knead the dough 8-10 times, until smooth. Add more flour to the work surface as needed until the dough is no longer sticky.
  • 4. Air Fry Preheat air fryer to 350 for 5 min. Spray basket with nonstick spray and air fry 12-14 minutes FLIPPING half way through, or until golden brown.
  • 5. OR Bake Bake in the preheated oven until puffed and golden brown, 25-30 minutes. Allow to cool for 15 minutes before slicing and serving. Bagels stay fresh in an airtight container at room temp for up to 4 days

    • Pan Fried Beer And Onion Bratwurst

    Pan Fried Beer And Onion Bratwurst

    Ingredients:

  • 4 bratwurst sausages
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter (or use olive oil, vegetable or corn oil)
  • 1 sweet onion (cut in halves or quarters and sliced)
  • 1 cup Belgian ale (or any dark, malty beer of your choice)

  • 1. Preheat pan over a medium-high heat. Add the butter and let it melt and start to turn light brown. The temperature should be about 325-350F at this point.
  • 2. Sear bratwurst for about 3 minutes per side, until it achieves deep golden brown color.
  • 3. Add the onions and the beer (or chicken broth), turn the heat to high and bring to a boil.
  • 4. Once the liquid is boiling, reduce heat and continue cooking, Cook for 5 minutes flipping the sausages and stirring the onions, cook for another 2-3 minutes until the internal temperature of the sausages reaches 160F. Cook uncovered to let the liquid reduce and intensify its flavor.
  • 5. Remove bratwurst from heat and serve hot, on a bun, or with you favorite side dish.
