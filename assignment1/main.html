Birthday Song

End of the Road - Boys II Men

The number one song of the first week in October in 1992 was End of the Road by Boyz II Men. Staying at the number one spot for 13 weeks in a row from the week of August 15 to the week of November 7th.

The song skyrocketed to the number one spot with the help from featuring the the popular Eddie Murphy movie Boomerang that came out the summer of 1992.

Formed in 1985, Boyz II Men are an American vocal harmony group from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, best known for emotional ballads and a cappella harmonies.
